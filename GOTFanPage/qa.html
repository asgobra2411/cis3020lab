How did you become a fan?

I became a fan after hearing friends talk about the intense storylines and complex characters. Once I watched the first season, I was immediately hooked.

What do you love most about it?

I love the political intrigue, character development, and unexpected plot twists.

What would you recommend to someone just getting started?

I would recommend paying close attention to character relationships and family houses. It helps to take your time understanding the world-building.

Do you have any favorite fan communities or creators?

I enjoy discussions on Reddit's r/gameofthrones and fan theory videos on YouTube.
